Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a station to Favorites?

Open a station and tap the heart icon. You can view favorites from the Library tab.

How is my listening history stored?

Listening history is stored locally and (optionally) synced to your account to enable cross-device history. You can clear history in Settings > Privacy.

App is buffering a lot — what can I do?

Try switching networks (Wi‑Fi vs mobile), close background apps, or select a lower-quality stream in the station settings.

Where can I request a new radio station?

Use the "Request Station" form below — provide the station name and stream URL. Our editorial team will review submissions.

How do I change app language?

myRadio follows your device language by default. To change manually, open Settings > Language & Region inside the app.

Report a Problem
